Inventory Management System

About Inventory Management System


This system is designed for wholesaler company which purchase items from the manufacturer and sale to the retailer in different places. So the company can have several warehouses to store inventory around the world.


This system can be used for six user roles: salesman, buyer, order handler, warehouse keeper, deliveryman, company manager. And it can simplify their work a lot.


Our Values


MISSION: Make your work more clear and easier.

Merits


Salesman

Input sales plan. Sign contracts with customer.
Manage customers' information. Create sales order.
Arrange sale order. Follow the sale order.

Buyer

Make purchase plans. Create purchase orders.
Follow the purchase order. Manage item and stock information

Financial employee

Pay money for purchase order.
Receive money from customers.



Warehouse Keeper

Inventory Inspection.
Warehouse Entry. Warehouse Exit.

Deliveryman

Collect stocks from warehouses.
Deliver stocks to customers. Confirm sales order.

Company Manager

View sales report.
View purchase report.
